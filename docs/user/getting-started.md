# Getting Started with Alma

> ## 🌸 Try Alma now → [https://alma-bot.com](https://alma-bot.com)
>
> Live demo, no signup, bilingual ES/EN. Running on Google Cloud Run.

## What is Alma?

Alma is your AI companion for emotional wellness. She listens, remembers your story, and checks in when you go quiet. She's available on Telegram and on the web.

Alma is not a therapist and does not replace professional mental health care. She's the friend who remembers to ask how your job interview went.

## Using Alma on Telegram

1. Open Telegram and search for `@AlmaCompanionBot` (or click the link provided by your administrator)
2. Send any message to start — try "Hola, soy nuevo aquí"
3. Alma will greet you and ask how you're feeling
4. Talk naturally in Spanish — Alma understands context, emotions, and follows up

### What to Expect

- **Alma remembers**: If you mention a job interview, she'll ask about it next time
- **Daily check-ins**: Alma sends 3 gentle messages per day:
  - ☀️ Morning (~8:30am) — "¿Ya desayunaste?"
  - 🌞 Afternoon (~1:30pm) — "¿Ya almorzaste? ¿Cómo va tu día?"
  - 🌙 Evening (~7:30pm) — "¿Ya cenaste? ¿Hiciste algo de movimiento hoy?"
- **No spam**: If you're already chatting, she won't send check-ins. If you're in distress, she pauses non-urgent messages.

### Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| `/pausar` | Pause daily check-ins |
| `/reanudar` | Resume daily check-ins |

## Using Alma on Web

1. Open **[https://alma-bot.com](https://alma-bot.com)** (or `http://localhost:3000` if running locally)
2. A unique ID is created automatically — your conversations are private
3. Type in the chat box and press Enter
4. Watch the memory panel on the right — it updates as Alma learns about you

## If You're in Crisis

Alma takes your safety seriously. If she detects you may be in distress:
- She'll ask direct, caring questions
- She'll provide crisis resources:
  - **MINSA Línea de Vida**: 113 (Peru)
  - **Crisis Text Line**: available in your region
- She'll offer to connect you with a human listener

Alma never diagnoses, minimizes, or gives generic advice during a crisis.
