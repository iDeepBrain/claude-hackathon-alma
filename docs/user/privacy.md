# Privacy — What Alma Stores

## What Alma Remembers

Alma stores four types of information to provide continuity across conversations:

| What | Example | Why |
|------|---------|-----|
| **Mood history** | "You were feeling 7/10 yesterday" | Track emotional trends over time |
| **Life events** | "Job interview at tech company" | Follow up on things that matter to you |
| **Habits** | "Sleeps late on weekends" | Understand your patterns |
| **Communication preferences** | "Prefers short responses" | Adapt conversation style |

## Where Your Data Lives

- All data is stored in a **local SQLite database** (`alma.db`) on the server running Alma
- Your conversation history is stored in **Redis** with a 24-hour expiration
- Memory embeddings are computed **locally** using ONNX — your messages are never sent to a third-party embedding service
- Conversations with Claude (Anthropic) are subject to [Anthropic's privacy policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)

## What Alma Does NOT Do

- Does not share your data with other users
- Does not sell or monetize your data
- Does not store raw conversation logs permanently — only extracted memories
- Does not use your data to train models
- Does not make clinical diagnoses

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention | Location |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| Conversation history | 24 hours | Redis |
| Semantic cache | 1 hour | Redis |
| Memory layers | Permanent | SQLite |
| Crisis scores | 24 hours | Redis |

## Deleting Your Data

Contact the administrator to request deletion of your memory entries from the SQLite database. Redis data expires automatically based on the TTLs above.

## Crisis Data

When Alma detects a potential crisis, the crisis score is stored in Redis (24h TTL) and the event is added to your `mentioned_events` memory layer. This is used to:
1. Route you to the most capable Claude model (Opus)
2. Suppress non-urgent proactive messages while you're in distress
3. Follow up after the crisis has passed

Crisis escalation resources (MINSA 113) are hardcoded — they are not generated by the AI model.
